About FOODS

Introducing Foods, your go-to user-friendly website, dedicated to helping you discover the perfect recipes. Our platform offers a seamless and convenient recipe search experience, with each result accompanied by a direct link to the original source. This way, you can easily access all the essential recipe details and instructions from the original provider.

We also take dietary restrictions seriously, ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience for all users. Feel free to explore our diverse collection of recipes while having peace of mind that your dietary needs are considered.

Start your culinary adventure with us and explore a wide range of delectable recipes. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a cooking enthusiast, Foods is here to enhance your cooking journey with ease and inspiration.

Dietary restrictions

Dairy Free

Any food that contains no milk or milk products.

Gluten Free

Excludes gluten, a protein found in grains like wheat, barley, rye, and their derivatives.

Vegan

This type of diet excludes all animal products and by-products.

About the API Edamam

Edamam API offers a vast recipe database, boasting over 2 million English recipes. They consistently update their collection by adding new websites and recipes regularly.

For this project, we specifically utilized the Recipe Search API. However, Edamam provides two other APIs as well: the Nutrition Analysis API and the Food Database API.
